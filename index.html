Hi! I am a PhD student at the Chair of IT Security at Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT).

Research Interests

My main focus lies on creating formal privacy notions for group-based anonymous communication. Once formal privacy goals have been established, present and future anonymous group communication networks can be analyzed.

I am also researching anonymous publish/subscribe networks in a joined project with the Telecooperation Lab at TU Darmstadt.

During my master's thesis, I conducted a formal analysis of the anonymous communication network Vuvuzela, in which I found and fixed serious weaknesses against sender timing and replay attacks. You can find the thesis here.
